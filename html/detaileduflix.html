EDUFLIX

DESCRIPTION

Eduflix is an online learning application designed to help users explore and master various topics at their own pace and convenience. From business and IT to design, music, and finance, Eduflix offers a wide range of courses tailored to diverse interests and skill levels.

The platform features a clean, friendly interface that allows users to register easily, browse through curated content, read detailed articles, and track their progress in enrolled courses. Each course provides visual feedback on completion percentage, encouraging learners to stay motivated.

Eduflix also includes a profile section where users can manage personal information, view purchased courses, and access their wishlist. Whether you're just getting started or looking to enhance specific skills, Eduflix is built to support flexible and engaging learning experiences.
