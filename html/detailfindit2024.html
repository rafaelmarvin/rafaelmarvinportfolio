HACKATHON FINDIT 2025 (WeCan)

DESCRIPTION

WeCan is a mental wellness platform designed to support Gen Z in their emotional and psychological well-being through a blend of gamified experiences and practical self-care tools. Developed using the MERN stack, WeCan encourages users to maintain a balanced state of mind through tasks, journaling, and personalized mood tracking — all while earning points as motivation.

This project was created for the FindIT 2024 Hackathon at Universitas Gadjah Mada, where our team successfully achieved 4th place. Our multidisciplinary team consisted of:

  • Joseph Christian : Hacker (Developer)
  • Alicia Felisha : Hipster (UI/UX Designer)
  • Rafael Marvin : Hustler (Business & Sustainability Lead)

As a team, our mission was to build a platform that felt warm, safe, and empowering for young users — especially in today's fast-paced digital world where mental health is often overlooked.

Key features of WeCan include: Interactive Dashboard: Tracks user progress and overall wellness score. Mood Tracking & Journaling: Allows users to log emotions and write reflections. Daily Quests/Tasks: Small achievable goals to boost mood and sense of productivity. Point-based Motivation System: Earn “Wellness Points” as a reward for consistency. Statistics & Insights: Visual charts that help users understand patterns in their mental well-being.

WeCan is more than just a mental health tracker — it’s a daily companion that promotes self-awareness and self-care in an engaging and non-intrusive way. By combining design, tech, and business insight, we aimed to make wellness accessible and relevant for the next generation./p>
